About the Film

"I Feel" by Hamad is a short film that reflects how we experience emotion through the places around us.

Shot entirely on the NYUAD campus, the film uses quiet visuals, ambient sound, and on-screen text to guide viewers through a series of moods and moments, without ever speaking a word.
